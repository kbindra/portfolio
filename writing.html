Mirror

Restlessness in her heart,
Chaos in her brain;
Her breast conceals bewilderment,
A cry her heart exclaims;

Unzipping fragments of soul,
She encounters diverging emotions;
Detachment from the worldliness?,
Or was affection her heart's devotion;
Sanity is what she seeks,
Absurdity is what is exposed;
Deciphering this maze is tricky,
How she wishes someone was close;

But that is not how it ends,
You come closer, she pushes you away;
She is afraid she might disclose her vulnerability,
But hopes you do not push her away;

Fighting those demons,
Isn't easy for her anyway;
Fight for her instead
You conquer her in that way.
